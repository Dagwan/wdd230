Visual Hierarchy

Kelly Peppers

Why are grids or flex useful in web design? The interesting thing to know about sites with 3 column contents and Why grids or flex matter in web design is that they help give shape and hierarchy to our web design pages. Without them, we would have no idea where to place our pages or design elements. We need them to create a good user experience so our user knows how to navigate through our website and find what they need. It is a good practice for us to always put our pages in a tide way so they can continue to give our client the better experience they would expect from us.
